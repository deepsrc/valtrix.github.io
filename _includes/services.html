Valtrix Systems also offers consultation and service on a wide range of design verification and system validation related tasks. These include, but are not limited, to:

PORTING STING TO YOUR TARGET SYSTEM

The architecture agnostic design of STING allows for easy portability. STING can be easily enabled by adding the drivers for the unique IPs and by porting the test generation algorithms to the target system architecture of the SoC. In case the IPs are already supported by STING, the tool can be enabled out of the box without making significant changes.

DEVELOP A COMPREHENSIVE VERIFICATION AND DEBUG STRATEGY

Our team comprises of IP validation experts and can help create comprehensive verification and debug plans for your IPs and SoCs. Test plans are provided for the functional aspect of the IPs. Scenarios are developed and graded according to their importance. We can also help review existing plans and address coverage gaps in it. Test scenarios in the existing verification plans can also be ported to STING or other Valtrix products.

VERIFICATION INFRASTRUCTURE

A good infrastructure is very important to achieve verification goals. Good design makes the entire process intuitive and does not cause distraction to the engineers. We can help you build infrastructure for verification that caters to the unique needs of your company. It will consider the in-house tools and interfaces to extract information such that it is easy to understand the overall status of verification in progress. All the data will be captured and reported in real time.

CONSULTATION ON HARDWARE DESIGN AND VERIFICATION

We also provide consultations on hardware design with a special focus on adopting the most optimal verification strategy for your SoC

STING RELATED SUPPORT OR TRAINING

Any support or training which will help the customer to develop a hands-on expertise of STING can be provided. All the salient features of the tool will be explained in sufficient detail along with demo exercises on test stimulus development.
